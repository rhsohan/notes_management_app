# Premium Notes - User Guide

Welcome to **Premium Notes**, a beautifully designed note-taking app that helps you keep track of your thoughts, ideas, and tasks with ease. Below is a guide on how to use the various features of the app.

## 📝 Viewing Your Notes
When you open the app, you will land on the **My Notes** screen. 
- All your notes are displayed in a colorful grid.
- If you haven't created any notes yet, you will see a prompt to "Create something brilliant!"

## ➕ Creating a New Note
1. From the main screen, tap the **New Note** floating action button located at the bottom right.
2. You will be taken to the "Create Note" screen.
3. Tap the **Title** field and enter a title for your note.
4. Tap the **Description** field and write the content of your note.
5. Once you are finished, tap the **Save Note** button.
6. Your new note will be saved and you will be returned to the main screen where your new note will appear.

## ✏️ Editing an Existing Note
1. From the main screen, simply tap on any note card you wish to edit.
2. The "Edit Note" screen will open, pre-filled with the current title and description.
3. Make any changes you need to the text.
4. Tap the **Save Note** button to apply your changes.

## 🗑️ Deleting a Note
1. On the main screen, locate the note you want to delete.
2. On the right side of the note's card, tap the **trash/delete** icon.
3. A confirmation dialog will appear asking "Are you sure you want to delete this note?".
4. Tap **Delete** to permanently remove the note, or tap **Cancel** if you changed your mind.

Enjoy using Premium Notes!
